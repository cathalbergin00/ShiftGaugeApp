Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

Overview

ShiftGauge is designed for pay-period planning. The app is local-first and supports core workflows offline.

What data ShiftGauge stores

ShiftGauge stores planning data you enter, such as shifts, pay period settings, goals, calibration entries, and certification records.

This data is stored on your device by default so you can continue planning without relying on a live internet connection.

What ShiftGauge does not do

ShiftGauge is not a payroll processor and does not change employer payroll outcomes. Estimates are planning aids and may differ from final payroll values.

Website analytics and links

The ShiftGauge website may record basic CTA click events to measure page performance. Clicking App Store links opens Apple-owned pages governed by Apple policies.

Contact

For privacy questions or account support, contact support@shiftgauge.app.
