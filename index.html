Why ShiftGauge

Payroll complexity should not block good decisions.

Rotating schedules, overtime rules, differentials, and side shifts make paycheck prediction hard to trust with basic spreadsheets.

ShiftGauge translates that complexity into a period-based planning view so you can decide with confidence before payday.
