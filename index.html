Why ShiftGauge

Payroll rules are complex. Planning should still feel simple.

Overtime, differential pay, comp time, and multi-role shifts can make spreadsheets fragile and hard to trust.

ShiftGauge translates that complexity into period-based clarity so you can make decisions early, with fewer end-of-cycle surprises.

FAQ

Questions people ask before they commit.

Is ShiftGauge a payroll provider?

No. ShiftGauge is a planning tool. Final payroll values still come from your employer payroll system.

Does ShiftGauge work offline?

Yes. Core planning workflows are available offline, with local-first storage by default.

How does estimate calibration work?

As you log actual paycheck outcomes, ShiftGauge can better align estimates to your real-world pay patterns.

Can I track overtime, comp, and side jobs?

Yes. You can capture multiple earning streams and review them together by pay period.
