Why ShiftGauge

Most shift pay is hard to predict. Your planning should not be.

Rotating schedules, overtime rules, and multi-role work can make paycheck timing feel uncertain. ShiftGauge turns that complexity into a clear, pay-period-based view so you can make decisions before payday, not after.
