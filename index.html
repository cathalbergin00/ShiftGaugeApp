FAQ

Answers before you download.

How does estimate calibration work?

We estimate take-home from period earnings, taxes, and deductions, then tune using real checks. Recurring holiday pay and differentials are folded into effective rates over time.

Can I track overtime, comp, and side jobs?

Yes. ShiftGauge supports multiple earning streams in one pay-period planning model.

Can I track certifications and renewals?

Yes. ShiftGauge includes certification tracking so renewal deadlines stay visible alongside shift and pay planning.

Is ShiftGauge a payroll provider?

No. ShiftGauge is a planning app. Final payroll values come from your employer payroll system.

Does ShiftGauge work offline?

Yes. Core planning workflows are available offline, with local-first data handling by default.
